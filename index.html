welcome to krypton!

browse the web freely. let's show you around.

search anything

type a URL or search query into the address bar at the top

tabs

open tabs using the + button at the top

bookmarks

click the star icon at the top to bookmark a page

offline mode

use the site online and play games at least once when online to use the site offline.

when this is done, go to the site offline, and the games you played online will be available offline.

tab cloaking

in the three dots at the top right, click 'Cloak' to cloak the tab into an about:blank page!
